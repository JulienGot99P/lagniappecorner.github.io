In 2009, Jay Z predicted the death of auto-tune, upsetting its self-proclaimed king, the Floridian T-Pain. Almost 10 years after, T Pain’s career collapsed, but his creature conquered rap music.

The popularization of Auto-Tune will be remember in the history of hip-hop as a major schism. Rarely an instrument divided the audience so completely. For many, the audio processor popularized by Cher and Daft Punk will always appear like a murder weapon, that helped merging rap and pop music, and dig the grave of R&B singers.

The despised instrument now resonates in every night club around the globe. A consecration for which T-Payne may not have received the merit he deserves.

Trying to revive his career, and apparently tired of sitting on his never-released collaboration with Lil Wayne, T-Pain followed the zeitgeist : he just dropped the thing for free on his soundcloud. It comes straight from 2009, when both of them where at the top of their game. Archaeological.
