Europe woke up to the smiling face of Jeremy Corbyn, the leftist hero of the day. Looks like conservatism suffered another defeat, defying the pessimistic projections of a continent falling apart under the strikes of gloomy nationalists. Could it be that we are allowed to begin this summer 2017 with fresh confidence and optimistic spirit?

Starting with listening to Jay Prince’s last mixtape Late Summers won’t hurt. It’s filled with jazz chords, laid-back ambient, and heartwarming lyrics. There’s even a little bit of sax. Like Jay said himself “it’s been a long winter”. Way too long man.
