In ancient Greek cosmology, the universe was conceptualized as an orderly system, where every existing thing was just right in place, with Earth it its center. The world of DC Comics is different: it’s a big mess where infinite Earths coexist in one physical space, separated by differing vibrations, and frequently rebooted by cosmological crisis. Connoisseurs refer to this as the Multiverse

.

Among the Multiverse, each Earth has its unique rules and specificities. In one of them Superman is a villain. In another Bruce Wayne parents are still alive. In another last, all superheroes are actual hippies.  

After watching Kodak Black’s video for the single Too Many Years, one idea struck me. What if our Earth was the one where the Joker is a young boy from Florida, trying to wipe his personality disorders through rap music? With the help of PNB Rock, he attempts to overcome prejudices, and proves it ain’t that easy to be a supervillain.

One question remains: on this Earth, who is Batman?
