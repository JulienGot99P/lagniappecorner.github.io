Is Gucci Mane finally on his way to being the big star the world has been awaiting for ? That’s what you might think watching his recent performance on NPR tiny desk concert. East Atlanta Santa proved his ability to distribute shots of happiness like nobody else. 

Watch him rap “First Day Out,” “Waybach” and “Last Time”, with Zaytoven on the key, and make up your mind. I personally never felt that much inclination to hug a convicted felon, singing about selling crack.

This time it’s for good. Gucci is going for it all.
