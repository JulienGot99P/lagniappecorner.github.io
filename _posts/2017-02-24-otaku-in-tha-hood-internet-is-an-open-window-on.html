Internet is an open window on the world, yet we keep looking through an eyelet hole. What if we could just get lost and wander, despite of the language barriers ?

オタク (or OTAKU) IN THA HOOD is a series of short videos produced by Black File, a Japanese rap program. From what I understood, each episode dives into some rapper’s everyday life, exposing their nerdiest side. A unique opportunity to visit messy apartments and take a glimpse at crazy collections, from sneakers to fishing rods.

My one and only year of Japanese evening classes doesn’t allow me to understand a single word. But the good taste that country demonstrates for art, design and lifestyle is self-explanatory. 

If any Japanese speaker hears me: this is a cry for subtitles.

BONUS

If like me you are fascinated by Japan, take a look at the web show toco toco, by Anne Ferrero, that allows artists and creators to present their source of inspiration. And it includes subtitles. It’s poetic, soothing, and I can’t think of a better way to get through a Saturday morning hangover.
