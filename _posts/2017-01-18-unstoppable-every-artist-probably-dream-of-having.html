Every artist dream of having the same viral buzz than Migos right now. Memes all over the internet, crazy live performances and a Donald Glover speech propelled their ultimate club banger Bad and Boujee to n°1 position in charts.

You probably bumped that song enough already. No good reason to not enjoy their live performance on Jimmy Kimmel, and:

  1. Imitate their dance move in front of your mirror ;
  2. Try to fill up all the blanks left by censorship with the right words

No doubt Migos’ madness ain’t over yet. Yesterday, they released a collab with Hoodrich Pablo Juan – I Can, on an epic souding production by DJ Spinz & Honorable C.N.O.T.E.

The motto of the song sounds like the perfect homage to a Barack Obama riding off into the sunset.

So my advice: whatever happens in your life, do it like Migos. Close your eyes and repeat.
